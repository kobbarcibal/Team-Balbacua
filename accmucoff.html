GENERAL INFORMATION


The Muc-Off Dry Lube works to penetrate deep into the chain links and ensure durable, smooth protection in dry, dusty weather. This is a wax-based formula that is perfect for dry conditions, and also contains added protection against contaminants. It will care for your chain and moving drivetrain parts to keep everything running smoothly and silently, reducing friction and consequently wear and tear.

Gear shifts will happen silently, increasing the lifespan of expensive components like cassettes, pulleys, and of course, your chain, which is especially important now that you can easily spend over $100 on a chain these days.

The Muc-Off Dry Lube will work equally well for road, cross and MTB bikes and parts. It can be applied to chain, cables, shifters and derailleurs. The bottle uses a pipette system to make sure you can apply the lube equally and consistently along the chain and derailleur parts. It is a biodegradable formula, so is safe for your bike and the environment.
